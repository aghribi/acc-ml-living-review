How to Cite This Review

Help us track the impact of this living review by citing it in your work

Standard Citation

APA Format 
AI/ML for Particle Accelerators Collaboration. (2025). The AI/ML for particle accelerators living review. Retrieved from https://ml-accel-review.org
IEEE Format 
AI/ML for Particle Accelerators Collaboration, "The AI/ML for particle accelerators living review," 2025. [Online]. Available: https://ml-accel-review.org

BibTeX Entry

Use this BibTeX entry for LaTeX documents:

@misc{ml_accel_review_2025,
  title        = {The AI/ML for particle accelerators living review},
  author       = {{AI/ML for Particle Accelerators Collaboration}},
  year         = {2025},
  howpublished = {\url{https://ml-accel-review.org}},
  note         = {Accessed: 2025-09-30}
}

Citation in Text

When referencing this living review in your manuscript, we recommend the following format:

"According to the AI/ML for Particle Accelerators Living Review [1], machine learning techniques have been successfully applied to..."

Since this is a living review that is continuously updated, please include the access date in your citation to indicate which version of the review you consulted.

Why Cite This Review?

By citing this living review, you:

  • Support the continued maintenance and development of this community resource
  • Help track the impact and reach of AI/ML applications in accelerator science
  • Enable funding agencies to recognize the value of open, collaborative reviews
  • Give credit to the many contributors who maintain and update this resource
